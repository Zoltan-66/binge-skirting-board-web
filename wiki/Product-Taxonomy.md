# Product Taxonomy

## Buyer-facing categories

1. Surface-Mounted Aluminum Skirting
2. Recessed and Shadow Gap Systems
3. LED Skirting Systems
4. Solid Wood Skirting
5. Stainless Steel Skirting
6. WPC Skirting
7. Trims and Architectural Profiles
8. Accessories and Fixings

## Codes

- `AS-*`: surface-mounted aluminium
- `SS-*`: stainless steel
- `WS-*`: solid wood
- `RS-*`: recessed and architectural systems
- `AT-*`: edge and transition trims
- `CT-*`: corner trims
- `WPC-*`: wood-plastic composite

Flagship series: `WS-TG`, TG Clip-On Solid Wood Skirting System.
