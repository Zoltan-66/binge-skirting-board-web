# Design Principles

The visual direction is **European Architectural Systems**.

- Technical clarity before decoration
- Calm architectural imagery with no text over busy photographs
- Product codes, materials, installation methods, dimensions, and downloads are easy to scan
- Inspiration and technical evidence are separate but connected
- Strong sample, catalogue, drawing, and RFQ journeys
- No retail pricing, marketplace badges, fake certifications, or generic SaaS styling

Reference patterns come from Schlüter-Systems, Progress Profiles, Intrim Australia, and Döllken Profiles without copying their brand identities.
