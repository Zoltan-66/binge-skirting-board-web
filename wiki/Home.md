## Project objective

Build a credible B2B product and enquiry experience for distributors, importers, architects, fit-out contractors, and procurement teams in Europe and Oceania.

The website is not a retail shop. Its primary actions are product discovery, catalogue and drawing downloads, sample requests, and qualified RFQs.

## Working rule

Do not publish a technical claim simply because it appears in domestic marketplace artwork. Claims must be verified, kept internal, or omitted.
