# Product Taxonomy

## Skirting boards

- Surface-mounted aluminium skirting (`AS-*`)
- Stainless-steel skirting (`SS-*`)
- Solid wood skirting (`WS-*`)
- WPC skirting (`WPC-*`)

## Architectural skirting

- Shadow-gap systems (`RS-SG`)
- Recessed systems (`RS-RC`)
- Flush-recessed systems (`RS-FL`)
- LED systems (`RS-LED`)
- Plaster-in systems (`RS-PI`)

## Trims and profiles

- L-angle edge trims (`AT-L`)
- F/C tile and stair trims (`AT-FC`)
- T/U transition and edge trims (`AT-TU`)
- Internal corner trims (`CT-IC`)
- External corner trims (`CT-EC`)

## Flagship product

`WS-TG` — **TG Clip-On Solid Wood Skirting System**
